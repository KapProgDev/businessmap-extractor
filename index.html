FAQ

Questions & answers

How does the extension work?
BusinessMap Extractor reads the Google Maps search results page and collects business data from each listing. It clicks through each result to get the full details including address, phone, and website.
Is the Free plan really free?
Yes! The Free plan gives you full access to business names, addresses, websites, ratings, and categories with no limits. The only feature reserved for Pro is phone numbers.
How do I get my Pro license key?
After subscribing, we email your license key instantly. Open the extension, click "Free Plan", paste your key, and click Activate. That's it.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, cancel anytime from inside the extension (click Pro Active → Manage Subscription) or email us. No lock-in, no questions asked.
What formats can I export to?
CSV, JSON, and Excel (XLSX). All formats are available on both Free and Pro plans.
How many listings can I scrape at once?
Up to 200 per session. For larger volumes, simply run multiple sessions with different searches.
